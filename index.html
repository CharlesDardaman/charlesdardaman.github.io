About

I am a security engineer focused on safeguarding complex systems, tracking down intrusions, and building pragmatic defenses. Over the past decade I have led incident response programs, advised product teams on threat models, and carried out niche research projects ranging from malware reversing to embedded systems triage.

These days I split time between hands-on security work, mentoring teams, and writing practical guides born out of real-world lessons. Off the keyboard you'll usually find me climbing, studying new hardware gadgets, or brewing very strong coffee.
